Pathanamthitta

The Land of Lord Ayyappa

About Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is known for its natural beauty and pilgrimage centers, including the famous Sabarimala Temple.

Key Details

  • Capital: Pathanamthitta
  • Important Railway Station: Chengannur Railway Station (nearest)
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: City Centre Mall, Mall of Pathanamthitta
  • Main Hospitals: Pushpagiri Medical College Hospital, District Hospital Pathanamthitta
  • Pilgrimage Centers: Sabarimala Temple, Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple
  • Best Hotels: KGA Elite Continental, Hotel Hills Park
  • Airports: Trivandrum International Airport (nearest)
  • Best Places: Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls, Gavi, Konni Forest Reserve, Aranmula, Pandalam
