Beaches in Kerala

Experience the pristine beaches of Kerala

Kerala, known for its stunning coastline, boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in India. From the serene backwaters to the vibrant beach towns, Kerala's beaches offer a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Whether you're looking to soak up the sun, enjoy water sports, or simply take in the breathtaking views, Kerala's beaches have something for everyone.

Popular Beaches

Kovalam Beach

Known for its crescent-shaped shoreline, Kovalam Beach is a popular destination for tourists seeking a mix of relaxation and water activities.

Varkala Beach

Varkala Beach is famous for its stunning cliffs and the ancient Janardanaswamy Temple, offering a unique blend of natural beauty.

Alleppey Beach

Also known as Alappuzha Beach, this destination is renowned for its picturesque backwaters and the vibrant Nehru Trophy Boat Race.
