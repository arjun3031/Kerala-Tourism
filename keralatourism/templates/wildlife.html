Wildlife in Kerala

Discover the rich wildlife of Kerala

Kerala, with its lush greenery and diverse ecosystems, is home to a rich array of wildlife. From dense forests to vibrant wetlands, Kerala's wildlife sanctuaries and national parks offer a glimpse into the region's natural beauty and biodiversity. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a bird watcher, or simply looking for a serene escape, Kerala's wildlife destinations have something to offer.

Popular Wildlife Destinations

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Located in Thekkady, Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is famous for its elephants and scenic lake. Visitors can enjoy boat rides, nature walks, and a chance to see a variety of wildlife in their natural habitat.

Eravikulam National Park

Eravikulam National Park, near Munnar, is known for its population of Nilgiri Tahr, an endangered mountain goat. The park offers stunning views, rolling grasslands, and a chance to see rare wildlife species.

Silent Valley National Park

Located in the Nilgiri Hills, Silent Valley National Park is a pristine expanse of tropical rainforest. The park is home to numerous species of flora and fauna, including the lion-tailed macaque.
