About Palakkad

Palakkad, also known as Palghat, is a district in the state of Kerala, India. Known for its rich cultural heritage, picturesque landscapes, and historical significance, Palakkad offers a unique experience for visitors.

Key Details

  • Capital: Palakkad
  • Important Railway Station: Palakkad Railway Station
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Sree Gokulam Shopping Mall
  • Main Hospitals: PK Das Institute of Medical Sciences, Malabar Hospital
  • Beaches: Not Applicable (Palakkad is known for its hills and forests)
  • Best Hotels: Kairali Heritage, Hotel Indraprastha
  • Airports: Coimbatore International Airport (nearest major airport)
  • Best Places: Silent Valley National Park, Malampuzha Dam, Palakkad Fort, Parambikulam Tiger Reserve
