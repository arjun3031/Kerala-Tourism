Kannur

A Coastal District of Kerala

About Kannur

Kannur, also known as Cannanore, is a coastal district in the northern part of Kerala. It is known for its beautiful beaches, historic forts, and vibrant cultural heritage.

Key Details

  • Capital: Kannur
  • Important Railway Station: Kannur Railway Station
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Galaxy Mall, Big Bazaar
  • Main Hospitals: Kannur Medical College, Aster MIMS
  • Beaches: Payyambalam Beach, Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach
  • Best Hotels: The Tower House, Hotel Olivio
  • Airports: Kannur International Airport
  • Best Places: St. Angelo Fort, Payyambalam Beach, Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach, Arakkal Museum, Bekal Fort
