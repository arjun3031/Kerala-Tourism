Ernakulam

The Commercial Capital of Kerala

About Ernakulam

Ernakulam, also known as Kochi, is the commercial capital of Kerala. It is famous for its port, historic landmarks, and vibrant city life.

Key Details

  • Capital: Kochi
  • Important Railway Station: Ernakulam Junction
  • Metro Availability: Kochi Metro
  • Main Malls: Lulu Mall, Oberon Mall, Centre Square Mall
  • Main Hospitals: Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences, Aster Medcity
  • Beaches: Cherai Beach, Fort Kochi Beach
  • Best Hotels: Grand Hyatt Kochi, Taj Malabar Resort
  • Airports: Cochin International Airport
  • Best Places: Fort Kochi, Marine Drive, Hill Palace, Mattancherry Palace, Jewish Synagogue
