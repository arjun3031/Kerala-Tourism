Alappuzha

The Venice of the East

About Alappuzha

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is famous for its houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters, a network of tranquil canals and lagoons.

Key Details

  • Capital: Alappuzha
  • Important Railway Station: Alappuzha Railway Station
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Alappuzha City Mall, Seematti Shopping Complex
  • Main Hospitals: Alappuzha Medical College, VSM Hospital
  • Beaches: Alappuzha Beach, Marari Beach
  • Best Hotels: Lake Palace Resort, Raheem Residency
  • Airports: Cochin International Airport (nearest)
  • Best Places: Alappuzha Beach, Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, Pathiramanal Island, Krishnapuram Palace
