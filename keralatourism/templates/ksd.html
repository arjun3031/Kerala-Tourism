Kasaragod

A District in Northern Kerala

About Kasaragod

Kasaragod is a district in the northern part of Kerala, known for its beautiful coastline, historic forts, and rich cultural heritage. It is also famous for its scenic landscapes and unique cultural traditions.

Key Details

  • Capital: Kasaragod
  • Important Railway Station: Kasaragod Railway Station
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Kasaragod Mall, Big Bazaar
  • Main Hospitals: Kasaragod District Hospital, Al-Badar Hospital
  • Beaches: Bekal Beach, Kappil Beach
  • Best Hotels: The Bekal Beach Resort, Hotel Comfort
  • Airports: Mangalore International Airport (nearby)
  • Best Places: Bekal Fort, Bekal Beach, Chandragiri Fort, Ananthapura Lake Temple, Kappil Beach
