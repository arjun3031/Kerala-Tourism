Houseboat Tourism in Kerala

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala's backwaters

   Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a paradise for nature lovers. The tranquil backwaters, lush green landscapes, and rich culture make it a perfect destination for a relaxing vacation. Houseboat tourism is one of the unique attractions of Kerala, offering an unforgettable experience as you cruise through the serene waters, witnessing the picturesque scenery and traditional village life.

Most Attractive Places

Alleppey

Known as the "Venice of the East," Alleppey is famous for its houseboat cruises and scenic beauty. It is a perfect spot to experience the tranquil backwaters and enjoy a relaxing stay on a traditional houseboat.

Kumarakom

A serene village on Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom is renowned for its bird sanctuary, luxury resorts, and houseboat cruises. Must visit place in Kerala. It is an ideal destination for bird watchers and nature enthusiasts.

Kollam

Kollam, also known as Quilon, is an ancient port city with a rich history and beautiful backwaters. A houseboat cruise in Kollam offers a glimpse of traditional Kerala life, scenic landscapes, and historical landmarks.
