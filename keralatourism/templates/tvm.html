About Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of Kerala. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic landmarks, and beautiful beaches.

Key Details

  • Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
  • Important Railway Station: Thiruvananthapuram Central
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Mall of Travancore, Lulu Mall
  • Main Hospitals: Trivandrum Medical College, KIMS Hospital
  • Beaches: Kovalam Beach, Shanghumukham Beach
  • Best Hotels: The Leela Kovalam, Hilton Garden Inn
  • Airports: Trivandrum International Airport
  • Best Places: Kovalam Beach, Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, Veli Tourist Village, Ponmudi Hill Station
