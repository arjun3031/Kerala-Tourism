About Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is a city in the northern part of Kerala known for its historical significance, beautiful beaches, and vibrant culture.

Key Details

  • Capital: Kozhikode
  • Important Railway Station: Kozhikode Railway Station
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Focus Mall, Inorbit Mall
  • Main Hospitals: KMCT Medical College, Baby Memorial Hospital
  • Beaches: Kozhikode Beach, Beypore Beach
  • Best Hotels: The Gateway Hotel, Hyatt Regency
  • Airports: Calicut International Airport
  • Best Places: Kozhikode Beach, Kappad Beach, Beypore Port, S.M. Street, Thusharagiri Waterfalls
