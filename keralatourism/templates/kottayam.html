Kottayam

The Land of Letters, Lakes, and Latex

About Kottayam

Kottayam is known for its literacy rate, picturesque backwaters, and rubber plantations. It is a place with rich cultural and historical significance.

Key Details

  • Capital: Kottayam
  • Important Railway Station: Kottayam Railway Station
  • Metro Availability: Not Available
  • Main Malls: Mall of Joy, Big Bazaar
  • Main Hospitals: Caritas Hospital, KIMS Hospital
  • Beaches: N/A
  • Best Hotels: Windsor Castle, Orchid Residency
  • Airports: Cochin International Airport (nearest)
  • Best Places: Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, Vembanad Lake, Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple, Elaveezhapoonchira, Marmala Waterfall
