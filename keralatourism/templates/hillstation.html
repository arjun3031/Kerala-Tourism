Hill Stations in Kerala

Explore the serene hill stations of Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is home to some of the most beautiful hill stations in India. Nestled in the Western Ghats, these hill stations are known for their scenic landscapes, pleasant climate, and rich biodiversity. From tea plantations to wildlife sanctuaries, Kerala's hill stations offer a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Popular Hill Stations

Munnar

Munnar is a popular hill station known for its sprawling tea plantations, picturesque landscapes, and cool climate. It is an ideal destination for trekking, sightseeing, and nature walks.

Wayanad

Wayanad is known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries. It offers a range of activities such as trekking, wildlife spotting, and exploring ancient caves.

Thekkady

Thekkady is famous for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, where you can spot elephants, tigers, and other wildlife. The scenic beauty and pleasant climate make it a popular tourist destination.
